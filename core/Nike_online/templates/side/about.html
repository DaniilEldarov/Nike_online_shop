Our Mission

We believe in more than just products — we believe in people. We champion equality, break barriers, and stand with athletes who use their platforms for change. That’s why we’re not just a brand — we’re a mindset, a drive, a culture.

To bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world.* And if you have a body, you are an athlete.

At Nike, our journey began in 1964, when a track coach, Bill Bowerman, and his athlete, Phil Knight, came together with a simple idea: athletes deserved better footwear.
